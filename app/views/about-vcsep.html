Voluntary and Community Sector Emergencies Partnership

The Voluntary and Community Sector Emergencies Partnership was founded in 2018, following the emergency at Grenfell Tower.

The aim of the VCSEP is to create an alliance between the voluntary and community sector to make it easier for grassroots and local organisations to get help during an emergency.

Our members include:

  • Victim Support
  • National Association for Voluntary and Community Action
  • Faith Action
  • Muslim Aid
  • St John Ambulance
  • The Salvation Army
  • The National Emergencies Trust
  • Business in the Community
  • British Red Cross
